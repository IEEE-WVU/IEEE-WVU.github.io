Build a CPU, in Minecraft!

Tutorial offered by The WVU Chapter of IEEE

Learn to design a CPU, from the ground up.

CPUs are complex and intricate.

Using Minecraft's easy-to-follow redstone, we'll design a simple CPU and explain how it works.

World downloads will be available after each session,
so you can experiment on your own.
